about

Hello! My name is Jarielle Catbagan and welcome to my personal website. Here I'll be showing my projects as well talking about anything related to robots, computers, science, technology, and the future.

One of the things about me is I really love robots! I find any kind of material on robots fascinating whether it be books or movies and I enjoy the excitement of participating in furthering robotics development in any way I can.

I am also a firm supporter of open-source development. It's amazing to see how open-source development has proliferated since the dawn of computer software and how it can truly redefine how we live our lives.

I have been involved with various forms of software development since my start with simple embedded systems. Majority, if not all, of my software projects are on my GitHub. Some of these projects may not have any usability or any immediate applicability, but these were projects that I have undertaken as part of a learning experience. Nevertheless, my software projects are open-source with the intention of helping others learn through my experiences and possibly build off of my projects to create something even cooler.

If I were to summarize what my life goals are, they would be building robots, contributing to the efforts in making humans a multi-planetary species, and to ultimately help build the future.
